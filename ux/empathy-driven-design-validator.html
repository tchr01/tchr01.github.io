Project Overview

Traditional design processes often overlook the diverse needs of users with different abilities, cultural backgrounds, and contextual experiences. This leads to products that inadvertently exclude significant user segments, reducing market reach and potentially creating legal risks.

The Empathy-Driven Design Validator uses AI to simulate user experiences across a wide range of personas, helping designers understand and address potential barriers before they impact real users. By generating detailed user perspectives with specific accessibility needs and cultural contexts, the tool transforms abstract diversity guidelines into concrete, actionable design insights.

More than just a compliance checklist, this system builds genuine designer empathy by providing immersive, AI-generated scenarios that reveal how design choices impact users differently. It shifts the design paradigm from a one-size-fits-all approach to a nuanced, context-aware methodology that celebrates user diversity.
