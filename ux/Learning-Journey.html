Conceptualizing Admin-Centric Learning Management

The challenge began with a fundamental question: "How can we make xAPI data actionable for learning administrators?"

Initial exploration: "Learning analytics overwhelm admins with data instead of empowering them. What if we could transform raw xAPI data into intuitive intervention opportunities?"

Key insights emerged about admin needs and learning management:

  • Data overload: Raw xAPI data is too complex to act on quickly
  • Time pressure: Admins need immediate insights to support learners
  • Context gap: Analytics often lack clear intervention guidance
  • Efficiency barrier: Manual tracking reduces time for actual support

Prototyping Intelligent Learning Management

The goal was to create an interface that delivers immediate, human-centered support at the exact moment it's needed.

Design Challenge: Transform complex xAPI data streams into instant, actionable guidance using everyday language that resonates with both admins and learners

Key design principles emerged through our focus on just-in-time support:

"I need to act quickly when learners struggle"

AI instantly detects learning challenges and suggests personalized interventions in real-time

"Technical jargon confuses everyone"

Converted complex analytics into clear, action-focused guidance using everyday language

"Support needs to be immediate"

Auto-generated tasks and resources ready for instant deployment to struggling learners

"Every moment matters in learning"

Just-in-time interventions triggered by real-time xAPI activity patterns

"We need to be proactive, not reactive"

AI predicts potential struggles and suggests preventive support strategies
